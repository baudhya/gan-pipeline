## Recommended Order

1. `khan academy partial derivatives` — 30 min, gets you reading gradient notation
2. `statquest probability` — 1 hour total across a few videos
3. `andrej karpathy backpropagation from scratch` — 2 hours, makes everything concrete
4. `yannic kilcher GAN paper explained` — watch alongside Paper 1 (01_GAN_Goodfellow_2014.pdf)
5. `yannic kilcher pix2pix` — watch alongside Paper 9 (09_pix2pix_Isola_2017.pdf)

Yannic Kilcher is the most directly useful — he reads the exact papers in this repo and explains every equation on screen.
